Foreword

This is the Book of Cronos II.

This is the Users Manual (Compiled Version) for CronosII an email application born of the frustrations of existing email applications and the coding aspirations of a small team of coders scattered rarely across the face of this big blue marble we all call home.

Cronos aspires to simplicity, efficacy,stability and most importantly; whatever else it's users want it to be.

The Book supplied with the tar ball does not contain developers info. Check out the sources for the guidelines and code. Visit the web-site for more information. Or drop us a line and let us know what you think.

 

Old father time.... he owes me much sleep.
