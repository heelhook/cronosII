Keyboard Shortcuts

Build a better Mousetrap

While a mouse or other “pointing device” is a wonderfull thing the simple fact is that many operations on most Graphical User Interfaces can be undertaken much more efffectively using Accelerators or Keyboard Shortcuts. While they are not as intuitive for most of us once learn't you wonder how you lived without them.

CronosII is no exception to this <pun> rule_ of _thumb </pun>.

We hope you find the Default Short-Cuts simple to use and remember but by all means take advantage of GTK and Roll Your Own.
